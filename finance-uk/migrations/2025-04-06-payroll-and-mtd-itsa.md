# 2025-04-06 Payroll and MTD ITSA Hardening

This migration seeds the deterministic layer for the first high-risk areas:

- payroll rates and thresholds that changed from 6 April 2025
- the HMRC guidance correction that mandatory payrolling of BiKs is 6 April 2027, not 2026
- MTD for ITSA phase 1 going live from 6 April 2026 for qualifying income above £50,000

The repo still keeps narrative skills, but any date-sensitive numeric fact in these
areas should now be backed by the local lookup tools.
