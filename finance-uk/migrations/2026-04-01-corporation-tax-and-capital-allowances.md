# 2026-04-01 Corporation Tax and Capital Allowances

This migration records the 2026 effective-dated corporation tax support needed by
the hardening plan:

- main pool WDA moving from 18% to 14% from 1 April 2026
- the new 40% first-year allowance from 1 January 2026
- s455 moving to 35.75% from 6 April 2026, currently stored as `announced`
  pending Finance Act confirmation

The local calculators intentionally stop at bounded, source-backed outputs and do
not attempt to replace full corporation tax software.
