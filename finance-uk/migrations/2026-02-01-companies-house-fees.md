# 2026-02-01 Companies House Fee Refresh

This migration records the 1 February 2026 fee changes in the local data store:

- CS01 digital filing fee increased to £50
- incorporation digital filing fee increased to £100
- DS01 digital filing fee changed to £13

It also captures the January 2026 guidance update confirming that the wider
accounts-filing reforms are delayed and are not live in April 2027.
