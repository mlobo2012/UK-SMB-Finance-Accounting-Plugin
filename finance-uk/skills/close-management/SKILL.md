# Close Management — UK Month-End Process

> This skill assists with close process management but does not constitute professional accounting advice.

## Standard 5-Day Close Calendar

### Day 1: Cut-Off and Data Gathering

- [ ] Confirm period cut-off (last business day of the month)
- [ ] Ensure all sales invoices for the period are posted
- [ ] Ensure all purchase invoices received are entered
- [ ] Import bank transactions via ~~banking (Open Banking feed or manual import)
- [ ] Download credit card transactions
- [ ] Confirm payroll has been processed and RTI submitted to HMRC
- [ ] Confirm pension contributions have been submitted to provider
- [ ] Lock prior period in ~~accounting software

### Day 2: Reconciliations

- [ ] **Bank reconciliation** — reconcile all bank accounts
- [ ] **VAT control account reconciliation** — reconcile output VAT, input VAT, and net liability
- [ ] **PAYE/NIC creditor reconciliation** — match to RTI submission and P32
- [ ] **Pension creditor reconciliation** — match to pension provider statement
- [ ] **Trade debtors control** — reconcile to aged debtors listing; review doubtful debts
- [ ] **Trade creditors control** — reconcile to aged creditors listing
- [ ] **Petty cash reconciliation** (if applicable)

### Day 3: Accruals and Adjustments

- [ ] Post accounts payable accruals
- [ ] Post prepayment releases
- [ ] Post depreciation entries
- [ ] Post payroll accruals if cut-off differs from accounting period
- [ ] Post holiday pay accrual adjustment
- [ ] Review and post revenue accruals (per FRS 102 S.23 — version-aware)
- [ ] Post lease accounting entries — operating lease charge (pre-2026) or ROU asset depreciation + lease liability interest (post-2026)
- [ ] Post FX revaluation entries for foreign currency monetary items (closing rate per FRS 102 S.30)
- [ ] Post intercompany entries (if multi-entity)

### Day 4: Tax and Compliance

- [ ] **VAT return preparation** — if this is a quarter-end month
- [ ] **VAT position check** — verify VAT treatment of all revenue streams:
  - B2B services to overseas customers: outside scope of UK VAT (place of supply = customer's country under VATA 1994 s.7A)
  - B2C services to overseas customers: may be UK VAT-able depending on supply type
  - UK domestic supplies: standard rated unless specifically zero-rated, reduced, or exempt
  - Mixed supply businesses: confirm partial exemption position
- [ ] **Corporation Tax provision** — calculate and post:
  - Small profits rate: 19% (profits ≤ £50,000)
  - Main rate: 25% (profits > £250,000)
  - Marginal relief: fraction 3/200 for profits between £50,000–£250,000
- [ ] Post deferred tax adjustment (FRS 102 Section 29 timing difference approach)
- [ ] **Business rates accrual** — monthly portion of annual bill
- [ ] Post CIS deductions if applicable
- [ ] Review equity movements — share-based payments, dividends declared
- [ ] Prepare management accounts pack (P&L, Balance Sheet, Cash Flow if applicable)

### Day 5: Review and Sign-Off

- [ ] Run variance analysis on key line items vs prior period and budget
- [ ] Prepare management commentary for significant variances
- [ ] Review trial balance for unusual or unexpected balances
- [ ] Obtain Financial Controller / FD sign-off
- [ ] Distribute management accounts to stakeholders
- [ ] Document carry-forward items for next period

## VAT Calendar Integration

| Month | VAT Activity |
|-------|-------------|
| Month 1 of quarter | No VAT filing activity |
| Month 2 of quarter | No VAT filing activity |
| Month 3 of quarter (quarter-end) | Prepare VAT return during close; file via MTD by 7th of second month following quarter-end |

## PAYE Payment Deadlines

PAYE/NIC payments to HMRC due by **22nd of the month** following the tax month (electronic) or **19th** (postal). Tax months run 6th–5th.

Employers with average monthly PAYE liability under £1,500 may pay quarterly.

## Accelerated 3-Day Close

**Day 1:** Cut-off, bank reconciliation, VAT control reconciliation, payroll posting
**Day 2:** All accruals, adjustments, tax provisions, remaining reconciliations
**Day 3:** Variance analysis, management accounts production, sign-off

Requires: automated bank feeds (Open Banking), real-time payroll integration, automated recurring journal entries.

## Sole Trader / Freelancer Simplified Close

1. Reconcile bank account(s) and payment platforms (PayPal, Stripe, Wise)
2. Record any missing income and expenses
3. Post mileage claims and home office deductions
4. Review outstanding invoices and chase overdue debtors
5. Ensure VAT records are up to date (if VAT-registered)
6. Review profit to date against tax thresholds
